Latest Version: The latest current version of my supercompiler is available here. It follows the design of a draft paper I am working on.

This is my effort to optimise Haskell. This project is also known as -O11, after the compiler flag in Yhc which will implement these features.

The IFL 2007 paper was awarded the Peter Landin prize for the best paper presented at the symposium that year, as selected by the programme committee.
